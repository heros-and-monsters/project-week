Deyr fé, deyja frændr, deyr sjalfr it sama, ek veit einn, at aldrei deyr:
dómr um dauðan hvern.

Cattle die, kinsmen die, thy self must also die. I know one thing which never dies:
the songs sung of each on dead.

― Hávamál - The sayings of the high one Stanza 77
